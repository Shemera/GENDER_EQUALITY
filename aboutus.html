About Us

We are a group of passionate individuals dedicated to critically examining the HeForShe Movement.

On this page, you'll find our analysis, perspectives, and an opportunity to participate in our survey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a world in which women and men have equal rights under the law, and full enjoyment of their human rights.

Our Core Values

- Promoting women's security and combating gender-based violence

- Women's political participation and influence

- Women's economic empowerment and working conditions

- National accountability on gender equality and women's empowerment

Our Goal

- A nation with empowered women and girls
